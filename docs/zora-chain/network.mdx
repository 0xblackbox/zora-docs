### Adding to MetaMask

- Open the MetaMask extension
- Click on where it says Ethereum Mainnet at the top
- Then click on **Add Network** at the bottom
- This will open a new window, then click **Add a new network manually** at the bottom
- Lastly, add in the configuration details listed above
