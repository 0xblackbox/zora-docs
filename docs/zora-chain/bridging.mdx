# Bridging

---

### Portal Contract

ETH can be bridged from Ethereum Goerli to Zora Goerli by sending Goerli ETH to the contract address below. 
It will take about 10-15 minutes for funds to show up on Zora Goerli.

[0xDb9F51790365e7dc196e7D072728df39Be958ACe](https://goerli.etherscan.io/address/0xDb9F51790365e7dc196e7D072728df39Be958ACe)
